Safety instructions before and after the spraying process

Before spraying

  • All types of food are covered and it is preferable to place them in a side room
  • The windows of the house must be fully opened when spraying the pesticide, and a complete internal ventilation flow must be maintained during spraying
  • Remove children, the elderly, and anyone who suffers from respiratory problems from the home before starting the spraying process.
  • When using a ULV device, it is preferable to leave the house for 24 hours and at least 8 hours.

After spraying

  • Keep the windows of the house open for at least another 2 hours after spraying
  • Allow the sprayed areas to dry completely before allowing anyone to enter
  • Clean any exposed food surfaces before use
  • Dispose of any unused pesticide properly according to local regulations
  • If any adverse reactions occur after pesticide exposure, seek medical attention immediately
